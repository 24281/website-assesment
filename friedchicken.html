The Secret Crispy Recipe

  1. Soak chicken pieces in seasoned buttermilk for 4 hours.
  2. Dredge in a mix of flour, cornstarch, and spices.
  3. Let rest on a rack for 15 minutes before cooking.
  4. Fry in oil at 350°F until golden brown and cooked through.
