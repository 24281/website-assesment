Crispy Buttermilk Tenders

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken tenderloins
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1.5 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder (the secret for extra crunch!)
  • Salt, black pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Place tenders in a bag with buttermilk and spices. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  2. Dry Mix: In a shallow bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and spices.
  3. Coat: Remove tenders from buttermilk, dredge thoroughly in the flour mixture, pressing to ensure a thick coating.
  4. Fry: Heat about 1 inch of oil in a skillet to 350°F.
  5. Cook: Fry tenders for 3-4 minutes per side. They cook much faster than bone-in chicken! Serve immediately while hot and crispy.
