Nashville Hot Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 large chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1.5 cups flour + 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • The Hot Oil Paste:
  • 1/2 cup frying oil (taken from the pot)
  • 2 tbsp cayenne pepper (adjust based on heat preference)
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp chili powder

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Soak chicken in buttermilk for 2–4 hours.
  2. Dredge: Coat chicken in the flour/cornstarch mixture, pressing firmly to ensure a thick crust.
  3. Fry: Deep fry at 350°F (175°C) until deep golden brown and the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C).
  4. Make the Paste: In a heat-safe bowl, combine cayenne, brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, and chili powder. Carefully whisk in 1/2 cup of the hot frying oil you just used. It will sizzle—this blooms the spices and creates a smooth, fiery paste.
  5. The Finish: Place the fried chicken on a rack and brush the hot oil paste generously over both sides. Serve immediately on a slice of white bread to soak up the excess spicy oil.
